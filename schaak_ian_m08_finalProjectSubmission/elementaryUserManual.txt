***Elementary User Manual***
Run the application. Enter what you were given about a chemical
atomic number will not have a decimal point

atomic mass will have a decimal. 
When searching atomic mass, you may need to round the answer down to 4 digits after the decimal point.
if no result is found with atomic mass, you may need to round off a digit going from 4 after the decimal point to 3 and so on

searching name and symbol will return something if it is actually an element. If it does not find anything check your spelling


hit search to look through the periodic table
hit clear to clear the results and the search bar.

hit exit to close the app when you are done.
